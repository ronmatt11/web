  • This is an ML project to understand and predict customer churn for a Telecom company
  • Data gathering, preprocessing, cleaning, and transformation using Excel, SAS and Python
  • Data visualization models to analyze the demographic features that lead to customer churn
  • Predictive model with 80% accuracy using logistic regression to predict customer churn
  • Developed a business plan promoting longer contracts and to check for customer satisfaction regularly
  • Insights: Churn rate decreases with increase in tenure and high monthly charges cause customers to defect
